Dance is the hidden language of the soul

Bellydance, more correctly referred to as oriental dance, is an Arabic expressive dance that has its origins in Egypt since ancient times. It features movements of the hips and torso predominantly. It has evolved to take many different forms depending on the country and region, both in costume and dance style; with the Egyptian styles and costumes being the most recognized worldwide due to Egyptian cinema. The Egyptian style with its traditional Egyptian rythms is popular worldwide with many schools around the globe now practicing it.

There are many physical and mental health benefits associated with bellydancing and some of them include:

  • Better posture and balance
  • Strong and toned muscles
  • Increased confidence
  • Unleashed creativity
  • Stress-relieving effect
  • Enhanced brain power
